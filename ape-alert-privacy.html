Ape Alert Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 12, 2026

Ape Alert ("we", "our", or "us") is operated by Esemby Properties LLC. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Ape Alert iOS application.

Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide the service:

  • Phone number — used to verify your identity and deliver reminder calls to you.
  • Reminder content — the text of reminders you create, stored securely to schedule your calls.
  • Token balance and purchase history — to credit and track in-app purchases made through Apple's App Store.

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you via one-time phone verification.
  • To place automated phone calls that read your reminders aloud at your scheduled time.
  • To manage your token balance for call credits.
  • To improve app reliability and fix issues.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party providers to operate the service:

  • Telnyx — for placing outbound phone calls and delivering OTP verification codes. Your phone number is shared with Telnyx solely to route calls.
  • Microsoft Azure — for secure cloud hosting and data storage.
  • Apple StoreKit — for processing in-app purchases. We do not store your payment information.

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below. Completed and cancelled reminders may be retained for up to 90 days for reliability purposes.

Data Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Authentication tokens are stored in your device's secure Keychain. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Children's Privacy

Ape Alert is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at esembyproperties@gmail.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
