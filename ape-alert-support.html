Frequently Asked Questions

I didn't receive my reminder call. What happened?

A few things to check: make sure your phone number is verified correctly in Account settings, your token balance is greater than zero, and your phone isn't on Do Not Disturb or Focus mode. If your balance hit zero, the call won't be placed — top up tokens and reschedule the reminder. If the problem persists, contact us below.

How do tokens work?

Each call attempt uses one token. If you set max retries to 2, a missed first call will use a second token on the retry. Your token balance is stored in the cloud — it won't reset if you reinstall the app, change phones, or log out. New accounts receive free tokens to get started.

My token balance didn't update after a purchase.

Go to Account → tap "Sync Balance" to manually refresh your balance from the server. If the purchase went through Apple but tokens weren't credited, contact us with your Apple transaction ID and we'll credit your account.

How do I change my phone number?

Go to Account → "Change Phone Number." You'll receive a verification call on your new number to confirm the change. Each account can change its phone number once.

How do I set up a recurring reminder?

When creating a reminder, toggle on "Recurring" and set the interval (e.g. every 8 hours, every day). Recurring reminders fire automatically on your schedule until you delete them. They appear at the top of your reminder list.

Can I reschedule a reminder I missed?

Yes. Tap any past reminder in the list and select "Reschedule." You'll be prompted to pick a new time. The original reminder stays in your history and a new one is created.

The call came in but I couldn't hear the message.

Make sure your phone volume is turned up during the call. The reminder message appears as the caller name on your lock screen before you answer — so you can see it even without audio. If audio is consistently missing, contact us.

How do I delete my account?

Email us at esembyproperties@gmail.com with the subject "Delete Account" and include the phone number associated with your account. We'll delete all your data within 7 days.

Is Ape Alert available on Android?

Not currently. Ape Alert is iPhone-only at this time.
